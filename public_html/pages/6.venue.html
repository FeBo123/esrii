Venue - Openbare Bibliotheek Amsterdam (OBA)

The 7th esrii conference will be held in Amsterdam at the Openbare Bibliotheek (OBA: Dutch for Amsterdam public library).
The OBA is the largest public library in the Netherlands and is located in the center of Amsterdam, just around the corner from central statio.

OBA

More information on the locations for presentations, posters etc. will follow soon.

How to get there

The easiest way to find your way around Copenhagen (and the rest of Denmark) is to use the excellent travel planner Rejseplanen either directly on the web or via the app from App Store or Google Play.

General directions

From the airport: take the train to Amsterdam Centraal. From there it is a 5 minute walk.

From Amsterdam Centraal you can walk eastwards and will reach the OBA within 5 minutes.

Busses trams, and metros stop at or close to Amsterdam Centraal (central station). From here you can walk to the venue.
